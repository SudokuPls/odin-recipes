Easy Chicken Parm

This recipe will teach you how to make a delicious chicken parm.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of skinless, boneless chicken breasts.
  • 1 cup mozarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Cooking oil for frying

Steps

  1. Pour oil into a skillet and put it on medium heat. Slice chicken breasts into desired thickness. Use a meat hammer on the breasts if desired for tenderness.
  2. Put breadcrumbs into one bowl. Put the egg and milk into another and mix well.
  3. Coat the chicken in the egg wash and dip in the bread crumbs.
  4. Check oil heat. Desired temperature is between 350 and 365.
  5. Fry chicken on one side until golden brown. Then flip and do the other. Repeat for all the pieces.
  6. Put cooked chicken on a tray. Top with tomato sauce and mozarella cheese. Place in oven on max temp broil until cheese melts.
